Our Working Process

How We Collaborate to Deliver Brand-Led Strategic Growth

Our collaborative process ensures we develop effective strategies tailored to your specific needs while leveraging your brand as a strategic asset to drive growth locally, regionally, and internationally.

Our Process Phases

Phase 1

Onboarding & Scope Definition

Establishing the Foundation
  • Initial consultation to understand your strategic growth objectives
  • Collaborative scope definition with key stakeholders
  • Project charter development outlining our shared strategic approach
Phase 2

Discovery & Analysis

Gaining Comprehensive Insights
  • Stakeholder interviews to capture diverse organisational perspectives
  • Market research applying proven analytical frameworks
  • Workshop sessions focused on uncovering strategic insights
  • Progress reviews to establish shared understanding of findings
Phase 3

Strategic Development

Crafting Effective Growth Strategy
  • Strategic options workshop exploring alternative growth approaches
  • Brand positioning development for target markets
  • Iterative feedback sessions to refine strategic direction
  • Executive alignment presentation for leadership buy-in
Phase 4

Implementation Planning

Translating Strategy to Action
  • Action planning for strategic initiatives across target markets
  • Resource allocation and timeline development
  • Responsibility assignment with clear accountability framework
  • Establishment of success metrics and KPIs
Phase 5

Knowledge Transfer & Support

Ensuring Sustainable Growth
  • Capability building for effective strategy execution
  • Progress monitoring throughout implementation phase
  • Strategy refinement based on real-world market feedback
  • Ongoing advisory support for continued growth

Our Working Principles

Collaborative Partnership

We believe the best strategies emerge from true partnership, co-creating solutions that leverage your unique strengths in all markets--whether local, regional, or global--while building your team's strategic capabilities.

Agile Methodology

We maintain flexibility while ensuring methodical progress through iterative development, regular checkpoints, and adaptive planning to address the dynamic challenges of today's business landscape.

Practical Implementation Focus

We bridge the gap between strategy and execution with an implementation mindset, capability assessment, and clear success metrics that work across different market contexts and business environments.

Client Experience

What to Expect When Working With Us

  • Deep engagement with your business and its growth potential
  • Collaborative challenge of assumptions while honouring your market expertise
  • Framework-based rigour with cross-cultural fluency
  • Brand-led thinking delivering practical strategies for sustained growth

Typical Engagement Structure:

  • Duration: 8-12 weeks for initial strategy development
  • Team: Consultants with relevant market expertise
  • Client commitment: 2-3 hours weekly from key stakeholders
  • Location: Flexible combination of on-site and remote collaboration
