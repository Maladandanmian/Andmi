What to Expect

When you reach out to us, we'll schedule an initial consultation to understand your needs and determine how we can best support your business. This conversation is an opportunity for both of us to assess whether we're a good fit for working together.

If we move forward, we'll develop a tailored proposal outlining our recommended approach, timeline, and investment. We believe in transparency and will clearly communicate what you can expect from our partnership.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you typically work with clients?

Our collaborative process begins with understanding your specific challenges and objectives. We then develop a customised strategy that leverages your unique brand strengths to drive growth. Throughout our partnership, we maintain regular communication and provide ongoing support to ensure successful implementation.

What industries do you specialise in?

While we have experience across various sectors, we have particular expertise in working with technology businesses. Our adaptable approach allows us to apply strategic frameworks effectively across different industries.

How long does a typical engagement last?

Most initial strategy development engagements last 8-12 weeks, though this can vary based on project scope and complexity. Many clients choose to continue with ongoing advisory support after the initial phase to ensure effective implementation and adaptation as market conditions evolve.

What makes Andmi different from other consultancies?

Our distinctive approach integrates brand strategy with business growth objectives, ensuring alignment between how you're perceived and your strategic ambitions. We combine analytical rigour with creative thinking, and emphasise practical implementation over theoretical frameworks. Our global perspective coupled with localised expertise allows us to support businesses across different markets.
